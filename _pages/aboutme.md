## Who is behind BioFlowBeauty?

My name is **Leonardo Olivieri Carvalho**. I am a biomedical scientist with research published in the *Journal of Cellular Biochemistry* (Wiley), where my work focused on cellular signaling and biochemical mechanisms relevant to human health.

Over the years, I noticed a recurring problem: the beauty and wellness industry produces an enormous volume of content, but most of it is driven by marketing rather than science. Ingredients are overhyped, treatments are recommended without proper evidence, and women are often left navigating conflicting advice with no clear foundation.

BioFlowBeauty exists to change that.

## What you will find here

Every article on this site is grounded in peer-reviewed research. I cover:

- **Skincare science** — what ingredients actually work, how the skin barrier functions, and what the evidence says about common treatments like chemical peels, microneedling, and retinoids
- **Women's health** — hormonal health, disease prevention, and the specific physiological factors that affect women differently
- **Nutrition and weight management** — evidence-based approaches to eating well, managing body composition, and understanding metabolism
- **Hair health** — the biology of hair growth, what causes loss, and what the research supports for care and treatment
- **Makeup and cosmetic safety** — ingredients to be aware of, what regulatory bodies actually say, and how to make informed choices

## My approach

I read the studies. I check the sample sizes. I look at who funded the research. And I write in plain language so you can actually use the information.

This is not a blog about trends. It is a blog about what the evidence supports, with honest acknowledgment of where the science is still uncertain.

You can find my published research at the [Wiley Journal of Cellular Biochemistry](https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1002/jcb.25124){:target="_blank" rel="noopener"}.
