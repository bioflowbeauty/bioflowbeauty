*Last updated: May 2026*

BioFlowBeauty is committed to being transparent about how this site operates and how your information is handled. This page explains what data is collected, how it is used, and what your rights are.

## What information is collected

This site may collect the following:

- **Anonymized usage data** through Google Analytics, including pages visited, time on site, and general geographic region. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics.
- **Contact form submissions** processed through Formspree when you choose to reach out directly.
- **Cookies** from third-party services including Google Analytics and Google AdSense, used for traffic measurement and advertising respectively.

## Affiliate relationships

BioFlowBeauty participates in affiliate programs including Amazon Associates and ClickBank. Some links on this site are affiliate links, meaning a small commission may be earned if you click through and make a purchase. This comes at no additional cost to you.

Affiliate relationships never influence which products are recommended or reviewed. All content on this site reflects honest assessment based on the available evidence.

## Third-party services used

- **Google Analytics** for anonymized traffic analysis
- **Google AdSense** for display advertising
- **Formspree** for contact form processing

Each of these services operates under its own privacy policy and data practices.

## Cookies

This site uses cookies primarily through Google Analytics and AdSense. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, though some site functionality may be affected.

## Your data rights

If you have submitted information through the contact form and would like it removed, you may request deletion by writing to contact@bioflowbeauty.com.

## Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated periodically. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

## Contact

For any questions related to privacy: contact@bioflowbeauty.com
